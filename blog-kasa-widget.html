Discover the Key Differences Between Kasa Widgets and Watt Widgets

Are you a Kasa user searching for more visually appealing way to manage your devices? Meet Watt for Smart Devices— the alternative to the Kasa widget. With its stunning energy consumption charts, device insights, and intuitive interface.


What Sets the Watt Widget Apart?

The biggest difference lies in the presentation and functionality:

  • Beautiful Energy Consumption Charts: Watt widget provides visually appealing and detailed charts for energy usage, making it easier to understand your consumption patterns.
  • Runtime Tracking: For devices that don't support energy monitoring, Watt widget tracks runtime, giving you valuable insights into usage behavior.
  • Real-Time Energy Data: The widget displays both current energy usage and today's total consumption, helping you stay updated at a glance.
  • Dynamic Status Visualization: Device status are represented using colors that grow or shrink based on energy consumption, offering an intuitive way to monitor activity in real-time.

Who Can Benefit from Watt for Smart Devices?

Watt is perfect for:

  • Kasa Users Seeking an Complementary: With detailed charts and runtime tracking, you gain a deeper understanding of your energy consumption.
  • Energy-Conscious Users: Track consumption, reduce waste, and save money with detailed insights.
  • Smart Home Enthusiasts: Stay informed with up-to-the-15-minute energy usage statistics.

How to Switch to Watt for Smart Devices

Getting started with Watt is quick and easy:

1. Download the App: Available for iOS, iPadOS and macOS, search for “Watt for Smart Devices” on the App Store.
2. Connect Your Devices: Login with your Tapo or Kasa account in just a few taps.
3. Set Up Your Widget: Customize the widget to suit your preferences and enjoy unparalleled control at your fingertips.
