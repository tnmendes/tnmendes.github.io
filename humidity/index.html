How it works

Humidity Window App combines trusted Apple Weather data with proven indoor air‑quality science to compute the dew point, absolute humidity, and mold‑risk index. Set your typical home hours and the app highlights the lowest‑humidity 2‑hour window so you can ventilate efficiently.
